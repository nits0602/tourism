We’re Open!

Here To Stay

In Torrance, some things never go out of style – like our resilient spirit, diverse makeup and good vibes. We’ve all been through a lot but we’re coming out the other side with a new and improved point of view. We know things will never be the same, but our outlook is sunny and we’re here for you to stay. Come celebrate what you love the most about Torrance.

Lodging

Our hotels are open and ready to welcome you! There are currently no restrictions and we are accepting both leisure and non-essential travel reservations. Rest assured that your well-being is of utmost concern and the highest safety standards are in place to protect the health of all our guests and employees.

Learn More

 

Foodies + Craft Beer

Ready to sway our way and revisit your favorite dining spots? All of our 400+ restaurants and 12 craft breweries are looking forward to providing you with excellent hospitality while adhering to the health protocols in place for our county. Due to the ever-changing nature of circumstances, please reach out to your establishment of choice for the most updated information, including hours and reservations. Also, learn more about Torrance’s unique Dine Alfresco Initiative.

Learn More

Outdoor Shopping, Arts & Culture

Get ready to rediscover your love of retail therapy and explore the many cultural experiences Torrance has to offer. Most retail stores, including outdoor spaces of shopping malls and outdoor swap meets, are open for business. Although, please confirm beforehand with individual businesses, as they may have different hours or protocols in place. There’s also many outdoor cultural wonders to explore – like Madrona Marsh, the largest vernal freshwater marsh in Los Angeles County.

Learn More

Travel Safely

Torrance is here to stay and we’re excited to welcome visitors back once again. For more information and resources on local businesses and services during this time, we invite you to visit our Frequently Asked Questions page. Be prepared that face coverings are required in indoor public spaces, healthcare settings, on public transportation and rideshare vehicles.

Learn More

Beach

Our pristine 1.5 miles of uncrowded coastline at Torrance Beach is open. Come enjoy the surf and sand while maintaining physical distancing of six feet from others. At this time, group gatherings or events, along with organized sports, are not allowed.
